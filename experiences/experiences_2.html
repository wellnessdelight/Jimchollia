Jeju Halla Arboretum
It is an arboretum built for collection, proliferation, preservation, management, exhibition, and resource conversion of Jeju's native plant genetic resources. You can see many native plants of Jeju Island, and there is a natural ecological learning center, making it a good tourist destination for families to come.
Homepage : http://sumokwon.jeju.go.kr
