Seongeup Folk Village
It's a village where you can see traditional houses. It is a historic village that has maintained its tradition since the 1400s, and it is a place where many relics are originally buried, so relics from the Neolithic period are also coming out. Here, you can enjoy the program by applying for a traditional experience program not only in the exhibition room but also with your family and friends.
