#Udo
Seohwaudo
It was built as a stone house in the 1970s and remodeled as a lodging house for Seohwado Island in 2021. The main building is a modern reinterpretation of the main structure of the stone house as much as possible, and you can find the stepping stones of basalt, palm trees, and stone walls.
Homepage : https://seohwaudo.co.kr/
Address : 75, Samyanggosumul-gil, Udo-myeon, Jeju-si, Jeju-do, Republic of Korea
Tel : 0507-1428-7280
#Seogwipo-si
Dosoon house
It is an accommodation facility that renovated the old building of Jeju Island, the stone wall and the cute old building of this house are great resting place for the first traditional trip.
Homepage : https://www.instagram.com/dosoon_jeju
Address : 69, Dosunnam-ro, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do
Tel : 0507-1479-0241
#Seogwipo-si
Broadleaved Guest house
It is run by a couple, who are also the authors of the book "Staying in an Old House," and is a space created by modifying traditional Jeju houses with 100 years of experience one by one. It is a space where the cozy feeling of grandmother's nostalgia and cute and sensuous interior props meet to give travelers excitement and rest.
Homepage : https://www.instagram.com/broadleaved_house/
Address : 44, Sangmodaeseo-ro 20beon-gil, Daejeong-eup, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do, Republic of Korea
Tel : 010-9074-9589
