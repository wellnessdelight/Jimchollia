World Heritage Festival
Jeju has plenty of World Heritage sites with the story of volcanic beginnings, but many are unknown. The World Heritage Festival: Jeju Volcanic Islands and Lava Tubes allows these unknown sites to get attention worldwide. This program provides tour of Seongsan Ilchulbong Peak, Manjanggul Cave, and Jusangjeolli Cliff. The heritage town program, wellness festival, and Walking Tour Trail programs are also offered by the organization.
