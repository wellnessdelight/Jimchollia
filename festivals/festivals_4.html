Seogwipo Chilsimni Festival
The Chilsimni Festival allows tourists to enjoy relaxation with the beautiful nature of Seogwipo. It is a festival where you can enjoy different traditional cultures and hang out with various people. There are several performances of the stories of the past, present, and future of Chilsim-ri meet. The Seogwipo Chilsimni Festival revives local traditions and provides a place of harmony where various people can participate and enjoy. Enjoy the beautiful scenery, the unique taste of Seogwipo culture, and the unique people of the region together.
