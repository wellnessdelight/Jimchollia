Jeju Fire Festival
The Keumneung Wondam Festival takes place at Keumneung Beach, which offers a refreshing emerald-sea view. The Keumneung Wondam Festival includes sea activities such as catching fish bare-handed and experiencing traditional ways of fishing. You can enjoy exciting sea activities with your friends and family. In addition, there are other festival programs such as taking a stroll along the Wondam road, orchestra concerts, catching gingi (crabs) and clams, and more. It is a great chance to experience Jeju's tradition memorably.
