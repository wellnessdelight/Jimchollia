Iho Tewoo Festival
The Iho Tewoo Festival, loved by residents and tourists, is known for restoring and sublimating Jeju's unique fishing culture into a festival. During the festival, tourists can experience various programs such as the Tewoo rowing competition, the Tewoo boarding experience, Wondang fishing, wind-surfing, folk song performance, etc. Have fun learning and experiencing Jeju's unique culture from this amazing festival.
